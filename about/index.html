Founders' note

It's said that the journey of a lifetime starts with the turning of a page. That couldn’t be more true for the pages within our catalog.

As digital nomads, James and Scott spent much of their early lives traveling the globe, exploring over 80 countries across 6 continents. Always finding their way home, they spent countless nights sitting around a table sharing stories and pictures of the people they've met, the places they've seen, and the cultures they've experienced.

It's those stories and those experiences that led them to create Traveling Twins. Fueled by their passion for exploring the world, they're now on a bigger mission – a mission to bring the world closer to home.
